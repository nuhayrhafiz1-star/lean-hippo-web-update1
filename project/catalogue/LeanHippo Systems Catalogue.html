The Architecture
Technical credibility

Every Lean Hippo system has four layers.

Four stacked layers, connected by signal lines. Not just "automations" — architecture.

Layer 1
Data Layer
Where the business stores what is happening: customers, sales, stock, suppliers, invoices, approvals, tasks, SOPs, conversations, and reports.
Layer 2
Workflow Layer
Where routine work moves through defined steps: requests, approvals, assignments, reminders, handoffs, escalations, and reviews.
Layer 3
Visibility Layer
Where management sees what is happening: dashboards, alerts, summaries, performance indicators, and exception reports.
Layer 4
AI Intelligence Layer
Where the system can answer questions, summarize information, detect issues, draft responses, suggest actions, and help management make better decisions.
