Section eyebrow / kicker — tracked uppercase micro-label, optionally with an oversized section number.

```jsx
<Eyebrow number="04">System Architecture</Eyebrow>
<Eyebrow tone="muted">The Control Room · Visibility</Eyebrow>
```

Sits above headlines on catalogue pages and above panel titles in dashboards. Tones: `cobalt | muted | white`.
